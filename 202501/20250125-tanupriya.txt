What is the goal of this article?
A comprehensive guide to essential PySpark operations.

Who is the target audience?
Machine Learning Engineers and Data Engineers working with big data.

What problem is it solving?
The article provides a hands-on approach to understanding PySpark operations. Also, it can act as a cheat sheet for MLE.

What key concepts are covered?
* Foundational operations such as filter, select, where
* Data Transformation operations such as join, broadcast, window
* Aggregate functions
* Tips about Performance optimisation
